Omelette

Description

Best natural breakfast, high protein

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Unions
  • Bacon
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Take a pan
  2. Add some butter
  3. Add bacon and onions
  4. Add tomatoes
  5. Add eggs
  6. Flip it after 3 minutes
  7. Wait 2 minutes
  8. Et voila
