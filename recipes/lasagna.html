Lasagna

Description

Also avaible in glutenfree and vegan, just use glutenfree sheets or meat alternatives. Proteinbomb: best postworkout meal for athletes.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Groundbeef
  • Mozzarella
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Nativ olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook bolognese
  2. Take a casserole dish
  3. Wash the sheets
  4. Cut the mozzarella in small slices
  5. Put some bolognese on the ground of the casserole dish
  6. Add some sheets
  7. Add cheese
  8. Repeating till the casserole dish is full
  9. 200 degrees in the oven for like 43,7 minutes
